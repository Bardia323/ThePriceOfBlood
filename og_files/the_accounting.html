Deaths. War-attributable direct deaths. Bronze: ancient Near East conflicts est. Colonial: slave transit mortality (2M) + conquest. Industrial: WWI 20M + WWII 70–85M + colonial wars. Modern: post-9/11 (Brown Univ. CoW) + Cold War proxy wars. All mid-estimates; CI ranges shown.
Blood price. $/L extracted = value the system receives per litre of blood it costs = (shadow_$/GJ × GJ/death) ÷ 5L. Not the cost paid — the value extracted.
