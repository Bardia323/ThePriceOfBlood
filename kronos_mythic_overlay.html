The substrate changes every 500–1500 years. Grain → wood → coal → oil. The name of the god changes with each culture contact event. The ritual form changes — from literal child-burning at the Tophet to metaphorical sacrifice via conscription and resource war. The visibility of the accounting decreases over time. The Bronze Age at least performed the calculation openly at an altar.
What Does Not Change
The equation: generative life must be destroyed to sustain the energy-surplus system that constitutes order. The cosmological justification: without the sacrifice, chaos returns. Apep swallows the sun. Tiamat reclaims the waters. The BIP collapses. This is always the argument.
