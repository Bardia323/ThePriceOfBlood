# The Price of Blood

A mortality economics archive — seven analytical documents examining the human cost of energy, economy, and empire through quantitative analysis.

## The Archive

**deaths_per_barrel = total_attributable_deaths / proven_reserves_contested**

This collection presents the mathematics of mortality, converting political violence into deaths per barrel, energy consumption into lives per terawatt-hour, and historical patterns into feeding rates measured in children per era.

## Documents

1. **The Blood Price of Oil** — Deaths per barrel calculations for oil extracted from war zones
2. **Energy Per Death** — Comparative mortality analysis across energy production methods  
3. **Kronos Feed Rate** — Historical child mortality patterns through mythic framework
4. **The Devourer** — Cross-cultural analysis of child-devouring deity archetypes
5. **The Escaped** — Statistical analysis of systemic survival and escape velocity
6. **The Accounting** — Economic ledger of civilizational resource metabolism
7. **BU — The Blood Standard** — Currency theory backed by human lives instead of commodities

## Usage

Open `index.html` in your browser.

The numbers are exact. The sources are cited. The accounting is complete.
